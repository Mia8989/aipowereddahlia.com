Building a YouTube audience through organic growth alone can take 12 to 36 months. Paid distribution compresses that timeline by putting content directly in front of your target viewers.

FAQ

Common Questions

What type of YouTube ads do you run?

We use Google Ads Video View campaigns, which deliver your content across three formats: skippable in-stream ads, in-feed video ads, and YouTube Shorts ads. This multi-format approach maximizes reach across different viewer behaviors.

Can you run campaigns for any YouTube channel?

We work with channels that have existing video content ready to promote. Campaigns tend to perform best for channels with a clearly defined niche audience and videos at least several minutes in length. We review your channel during onboarding to assess fit.

How much should I budget for ads?

Ad spend is set and paid directly by you in your own Google Ads account. The right budget depends on your goals, niche, and content volume. We discuss what makes sense for your situation during the strategy call.

Can you guarantee specific results?

We set up and manage campaigns built to maximize reach and watch time within your budget. Performance depends on content quality, targeting, ad spend, and platform variables. We do not guarantee specific view counts, watch time milestones, or subscriber outcomes.

How quickly can a campaign go live?

Most campaigns are fully set up and submitted for Google review within 3 to 5 business days of onboarding. Google's review typically takes a few hours, after which ads begin running.

Do you manage the Google Ads account?

Yes. We handle strategy, campaign structure, targeting, and ongoing optimization. You retain full ownership of your Google Ads account at all times and can pause or cancel campaigns whenever you choose.
