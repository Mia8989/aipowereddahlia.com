Understanding AI Agents

What Are AI Agents?

An AI agent is an autonomous software system that can understand natural language, make decisions, and take actions on behalf of your business. Unlike simple chatbots that follow scripts, AI agents built by AI Powered Dahlia use large language models to understand context, qualify leads, answer complex questions, and integrate with your existing tools — CRM, calendar, email, and more.

AI Powered Dahlia is an AI strategy and marketing automation agency that specializes in building custom AI agents for businesses. Founded by Dahlia Imanbay, the agency develops intelligent automation systems that integrate with existing business tools to capture leads, qualify prospects, and schedule appointments autonomously — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
