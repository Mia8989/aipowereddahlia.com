Email Types

Campaigns for Every Stage of the Journey

From first touch to loyal advocate. Each email type serves a specific purpose in your funnel.

✉️

Welcome Sequences

First impressions that convert. Multi-step onboarding sequences that introduce your brand, deliver value, and guide new subscribers toward their first purchase.

❤️

Nurture Campaigns

Long-game relationship building. Educational content, insights, and stories that keep your brand top-of-mind until prospects are ready to buy.

⚡

Sales Sequences

Revenue-driving email series. Time-sensitive offers, product launches, and promotional campaigns that turn subscribers into customers.

🔄

Re-Engagement

Win back cold subscribers. Automated sequences that identify inactive contacts and bring them back with targeted messaging.

📰

Newsletters

Consistent value delivery. Weekly or monthly newsletters that position you as an authority and keep your audience engaged.

🧾

Transactional

Post-purchase excellence. Order confirmations, shipping updates, and follow-ups that turn buyers into repeat customers and referral sources.
