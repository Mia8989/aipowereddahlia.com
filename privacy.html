Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Who We Are

AI Powered Dahlia ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI strategy and marketing automation agency operated by Dahlia Imanbay. Our website is aipowereddahlia.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Book a strategy call through our scheduling tool
  • Fill out a contact or inquiry form
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or email list
  • Interact with us via email or social media

This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any details you share about your business needs.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website or source
  • General geographic location (city/country level)

We use privacy-focused analytics (Fathom Analytics) that does not use cookies or track personal data. No personally identifiable information is collected through our analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule calls
  • Provide the services you request
  • Send relevant updates if you opted in to communications
  • Improve our website and service offerings

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Calendly for scheduling calls
  • Mailchimp for email communications
  • Fathom Analytics for privacy-focused website analytics
  • Stripe for payment processing

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We use Fathom Analytics, which is cookie-free and compliant with GDPR, ePrivacy, PECR, and CCPA.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, reach out to us through our contact page.

This policy may be updated from time to time. We will note the date of the latest revision at the top of this page.
